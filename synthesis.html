01. Studio Protocol

  • 1
    The Environment Record in a quiet, padded space like a walk-in closet or a parked car. Do not record in an empty office or kitchen. Echo is the enemy of AI.
  • 2
    The Hardware Use the "Voice Memos" app on your iPhone. Hold the phone normally against your ear like a phone call. Absolutely no AirPods or Bluetooth headsets.
  • 3
    The Delivery Read the teleprompter script below exactly how you would speak to a high-end client across the table. Take natural breaths. Don't force a "radio voice."

02. Teleprompter Script

[Energetic & Welcoming]

Hey there, this is just a quick audio sample to train my digital voice clone. I want this to sound exactly like me on my best day—professional, engaging, but completely authentic.

[Serious & Authoritative]

Over the last twelve months, we've seen a massive shift in the market. Inventory is tightening, interest rates are fluctuating, and buyers are becoming incredibly strategic. You need an expert in your corner to navigate this.

[Warm & Conversational]

So, whether you're looking to buy your first home, sell a luxury estate, or just grab a coffee and talk about the neighborhood, I'm here to help. Reach out anytime. I'd love to connect.
